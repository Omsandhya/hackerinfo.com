Hacker Types

White Hat Hacker
White hats are hacker who work to keep data safe from other hacker by finding system vulnerability that can be mitigated.
White hats are usally employed by the target system's owner and are typically paid (something quite well) for their work.
Their work is not illegal because it is done with the system owner's contsent.

Black Hats Hacker
Black hats or crackers are hacker woth malicious intentions. They often steal, exploit, and sell data, and are usally motivated by
personal gain. Their work is usually illegal. A Cracker is like a black hat hacker, but is specifically someone who is very skilled and tries via hacking to make profits or to benefits, not just to vandalize.
Cracker find exolits for system vulnerabilities and often use them to theit advantage bu either selling the fix to the syste, owner
or selling the exploit to other black hat hackers, who is turn use it to steal information or gain royalities.

Grey Hat Hacker
A grey hat is a computer hacker or computersecurity expert who may sometimes violate laws or a typical
ethical standards, but does not have the malicious intent typical of a black hat hacker.
